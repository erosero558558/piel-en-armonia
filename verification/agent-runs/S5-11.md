# S5-11 — Follow-up post-consulta 48h

## Resumen

Se agregó el seguimiento automático post-consulta por WhatsApp en [lib/LeadOpsService.php](/Users/luciaguadalupecaizasanchez/Documents/GitHub/Aurora-Derm/lib/LeadOpsService.php), conectado al cron diario en [cron.php](/Users/luciaguadalupecaizasanchez/Documents/GitHub/Aurora-Derm/cron.php). Para persistir ese candado, se amplió el modelo de citas en [lib/models.php](/Users/luciaguadalupecaizasanchez/Documents/GitHub/Aurora-Derm/lib/models.php). La cobertura quedó en [tests/Integration/PostConsultationFollowUpCronTest.php](/Users/luciaguadalupecaizasanchez/Documents/GitHub/Aurora-Derm/tests/Integration/PostConsultationFollowUpCronTest.php), revalidando además [tests/Integration/AppointmentReminderCronTest.php](/Users/luciaguadalupecaizasanchez/Documents/GitHub/Aurora-Derm/tests/Integration/AppointmentReminderCronTest.php) y [tests/Integration/BirthdayReminderCronTest.php](/Users/luciaguadalupecaizasanchez/Documents/GitHub/Aurora-Derm/tests/Integration/BirthdayReminderCronTest.php).
