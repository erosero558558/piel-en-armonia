    Frequently asked questions

    Q1

    What should I bring to the visit?

    Bring your current medications, allergy history, previous treatments, and recent photos. That baseline lets us decide faster and with better context.

    Q2

    Do I need tests before attending?

    Only when they add a concrete decision point. If needed, tests are requested after the initial clinical evaluation.

    Q3

    When is early progression reviewed?

    First follow-up is commonly set between weeks 3 and 6, or sooner if pain, bleeding, or rapid worsening appears.
