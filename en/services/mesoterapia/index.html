    Questions people ask

    Q1

    Is downtime needed?

    Mild redness may appear for 24 to 48 hours, then most patients return to routine.

    Q2

    How often is it repeated?

    A common plan is 3 to 5 sessions spaced every 3 to 4 weeks.

    Q3

    Which services combine best with it?

    It often pairs well with medical hydration, gentle peels, or biostimulators once skin is stable.
