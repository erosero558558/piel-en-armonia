    Frequently asked questions

    Q1

    How many days does recovery usually take?

    Recovery usually lasts three to seven days, depending on peel depth and how your skin responds.

    Q2

    Can it be combined with laser?

    It can, but only when session timing protects the skin and keeps irritation from stacking up.

    Q3

    Which situations make us wait?

    We wait when there is active irritation, pregnancy, or little chance of keeping sun protection well.
