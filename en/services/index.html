    Follow-up

    Document comparable change

    Photos taken with similar light and distance help us decide whether this route is working or should shift.

    See service >

    Relapse

    Reopen the route when the flare returns

    If inflammation or symptoms come back, do not restart from zero. Re-enter through the closest route and adjust the next move.

    See service >

    Family

    Align who supports the care

    In pediatric or longer routes it helps to align home, school, and follow-up so the plan can actually hold.

    See service >

    Warning sign

    Do not delay a changing lesion

    If a mole, spot, or lesion changed quickly, that route is no longer watch-and-wait. It needs prompt review.

    See service >

    Procedure

    Confirm whether technology really adds value

    Before moving into laser or peels, it helps to confirm goal, recovery, and safety for your skin.
