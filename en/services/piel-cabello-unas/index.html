    Questions people ask

    Q1

    Which cases fit this service?

    This service includes mixed cases: hair shedding, nail changes, and skin symptoms appearing together.

    Q2

    Can this start through telemedicine?

    Yes. Telemedicine works as first triage and defines when direct in-clinic examination is needed.

    Q3

    How often should follow-up be scheduled?

    First follow-up is commonly planned in 4 to 8 weeks based on findings and early response.
