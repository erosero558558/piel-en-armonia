Frequently asked questions

Q1

When does the effect appear?

Initial softening appears around days 3 to 5, with full effect commonly seen by days 10 to 14.

Q2

How often is it usually repeated?

Average duration is 3 to 4 months, varying with metabolism, area, and dosing strategy.

Q3

What care should be maintained after treatment?

Avoid intense massage and strenuous training for 24 hours, and follow all aftercare instructions.
