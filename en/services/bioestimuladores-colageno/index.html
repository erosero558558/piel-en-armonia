    Frequently asked questions

    Q1

    How long until changes are visible?

    Change is progressive, often starting around week 6 and consolidating over 3 to 6 months.

    Q2

    How many sessions are commonly suggested?

    Most plans use 2 or 3 sessions, defined by laxity level, treated zones, and tissue response.

    Q3

    Can this combine with medical botox?

    Yes, it combines well within an integrated facial plan with clear timing and supervision.
