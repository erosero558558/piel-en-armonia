    01

    How it works

    • You share key history and photos with enough context to guide an initial review.
    • We prioritize risk to decide whether the case can be handled remotely.
    • We send an initial plan, clear care steps, and a follow-up window when needed.
    • If warning signs appear, we speed the move into clinic.
    See options >

    02

    When it usually fits

    • Previously assessed inflammatory flares that need an adjustment
    • Follow-up on active treatment plans after a recent change
    • Questions about how things are evolving between reviews
    • Initial guidance before arranging a clinic visit
    See options >

    03

    When clinic review makes more sense

    • Rapidly changing, bleeding, or non-healing lesions
    • Intense pain, accelerated spread, or functional impact
    • Insufficient response to the first remote plan
    • Need for procedures or direct diagnostic examination
