    How it works

    • You share history, symptoms, and photos taken as clearly as possible.
    • We review whether the problem can be guided remotely or really needs direct examination.
    • You receive an initial recommendation, basic care, and a reasonable follow-up window.
    • If warning signs appear, we bring clinic review forward.

    When it often helps

    • Known flares that need adjustment
    • Questions between scheduled reviews
    • First guidance before traveling in
    • Follow-up for active treatment with recent change

    03

    When clinic review makes more sense

    • Lesions that change quickly or bleed
    • Strong pain or marked worsening
    • Poor response to the first plan
    • Need for direct examination or procedure
